# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 23, 2026*

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Telegram bot operated by **Dade.Studio** ("the Bot", "we", "us") collects from you, why we collect it, and what we do with it. By starting a chat with the Bot or using any of its commands, you agree to this policy.

## 1. Who operates the Bot
Dade.Studio, a solo studio based in Colorado, USA. Privacy questions: **privacy@dade.studio**.

## 2. Information we collect
- **Telegram account identifiers:** numeric user ID, username (if set), first/last name (if set), language code.
- **Chat identifiers:** chat ID for the conversation with the Bot.
- **Message content:** text, commands, and attachments you send to the Bot.
- **Interaction metadata:** timestamps, command names, and interaction outcomes.

We do **not** collect your phone number, email, contact list, or location.

## 3. How we use your information
- Deliver the Bot's features and respond to messages.
- Route prompts to AI models powering the Bot (see §4).
- Debug errors, prevent abuse, and improve the Bot.
- Maintain a short operational log for reliability and rate-limiting.

We do not sell or rent your data.

## 4. Third-party services
- **Telegram Messenger Inc.** — message delivery layer. See https://telegram.org/privacy.
- **KiloClaw / Kilo Code** — agent runtime orchestrating the Bot.
- **OpenRouter** and underlying AI providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google) — receive prompt text to generate responses.
- **Hosting and logging infrastructure** operated by Dade.Studio.

## 5. Data retention
- **Message content:** up to 30 days in operational logs, then deleted or anonymized.
- **Error logs and aggregate stats** (no message content): up to 12 months.
- **Telegram user ID:** may be retained indefinitely if tied to a ban, abuse report, or persistent Bot setting.

## 6. Your rights
- **Stop using the Bot:** block it in Telegram.
- **Request deletion** of your data: email **privacy@dade.studio** or send `/deletemydata` to the Bot.
- **Request a copy** of your data: same email.

We respond to verified requests within 30 days.

## 7. Children
Not intended for users under 13. Email us if you believe a child has used the Bot and we will delete the associated data.

## 8. Security
Encrypted transport (HTTPS), access-controlled hosting, and least-privilege API keys. No system is perfectly secure.

## 9. International users
Operated from the United States. Data may be processed in the US and in regions where our third-party providers operate.

## 10. Changes to this policy
The "Last updated" date always reflects the current version. Material changes will be announced in the Bot before they take effect.

## 11. Contact
